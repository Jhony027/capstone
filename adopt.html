Our Rescued

At the Pawssion Project shelters in Bacolod and Bulacan, hundreds of rescues are waiting to find their fur-ever homes. Many of our Adoptables have been rescued from death row, from local pounds or from abusive environments. They are all vaccinated, dewormed and neutered and can be visited anytime, just with prior registration.
