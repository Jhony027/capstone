WE WANT YOU IN OUR TEAM!

We’ve always had struggles with manpower ever since Pawssion Project started last 2018. Luckily, our team is now slowly growing and we are only just getting started! As our community grows in number, we are always in need of volunteers. We have so many ideas in mind that are just so so difficult to execute right now due to lack of manpower. Imagine what more we could do if more people join our team! Let us all continue working together for the voiceless. We can all do something to help our furry friends!
